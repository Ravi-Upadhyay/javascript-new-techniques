Social-Groups

Make social groups, given a matrix representing friendship of two individuals.

PROBLEM STATEMENT: We have one matrix M of order (m x n), if we have 1 in matrix corresponding to (m,n) then m and n are friends. if we have 0 in matrix corresponding to (m,n) then m and n are not friends. We have to make groups out of it in which all people who are direct friends and indirect friends will be in same group.
